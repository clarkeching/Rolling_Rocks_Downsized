## About This Vault

**Author:** Clarke Ching
**Setting:** Watt's Bridge, Scotland (fictional)
**Time span:** August 3rd to November 18th (about 3.5 months)
**Core conflict:** Deliver an "impossible" software project (FPP) ahead of a competitor while learning that quality and speed aren't trade-offs

### Main Characters
- **[[Steve Abernethy|Steve Abernethy]]** - Development manager, protagonist. Early forties, widower with two daughters.
- **[[Craig Lally|Craig Lally]]** - Flow Master from TCQ who teaches thinking tools. Manufacturing background.
- **[[Cheryl|Cheryl]]** - Cafeteria manager who demonstrates the principles through her team's transformation.

### Key Themes
1. Quality and speed are not trade-offs
2. [[Small Batches|Small batches]] enable flexibility and quality
3. [[Late vs In Process Inspection|Build quality in]], don't inspect it in
4. The [[Inverted Pyramid|Inverted Pyramid]] - deliver value early and often
5. Each delivery must be [[Good Enough to Ship|good enough to ship]]
