# Steven J. Abernethy

## Overview
**Role:** Development Manager at Wyxcomb Financials (Wyx-Fin), Protagonist

## Description
**Physical:** Early forties. Has lived in Sweden and Canada during his career. Lives in a two-hundred-year-old stone gatehouse with round turrets that tourists stop to photograph.

**Personality:** Dedicated father raising two daughters after his wife's death. Initially resistant to asking for help, tends to firefight problems. Dismisses information that conflicts with his beliefs. Transforms into a humble learner who discovers the Inverted Pyramid approach himself.

## What They Represent
The experienced IT professional who must unlearn conventional wisdom to discover that quality and speed aren't trade-offs.

## Background
Joined Wyxcomb Group nineteen years earlier as a graduate programmer, risen through the company. Just over two years in current role managing nearly two hundred staff including contractors. Wife Fran passed away two-and-a-half years earlier from a brain aneurysm while they were vacationing in Rome.

## Family
Daughters Ashley (7, the eldest, does coloring books) and Alison (about 4, blonde hair). Mother took early retirement and moved in after Fran's death. Cat named Mr. Purrrrple.
