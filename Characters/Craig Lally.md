# Alistair Craig Lally

## Overview
**Role:** Flow Master at TCQ (Total Customer Quality), Steve's Mentor

## Description
**Physical:** Late-50s, tieless, wears a functional grey suit. Looks like a man who'd climbed a lot of mountains for pleasure and drives a twenty-year-old Volvo he maintains himself. Walks with a limp.

**Personality:** Patient teacher who plants ideas rather than forcing solutions. Uses techniques like paradoxical instructions ('try not to think about small batches') to help people discover insights themselves.

## What They Represent
The wise mentor who helps the protagonist see beyond conventional wisdom. Manufacturing background gives him fresh perspective on software problems.

## Background
Worked most of his life in manufacturing, never written a line of code. Resume includes Lean, Six Sigma, TOC. Has half a dozen patents to his name. Joined TCQ three years ago after his factory was offshored to Indonesia. His wife threatened to leave him if he took early retirement. Corner office with genuine sea view.

## Family
Married (wife mentioned)
