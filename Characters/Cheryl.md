# Cheryl

## Overview
**Role:** Cafeteria Manager at Wyx-Fin

## Description
**Physical:** Thin, short, middle-aged woman with a strong grip.

**Personality:** Direct and practical. Down-to-earth wisdom embodied. Uses concrete, memorable examples to teach profound principles.

## What They Represent
The unexpected teacher who provides wisdom through practical experience. Shows that profound principles can be demonstrated in humble settings.

## Background
Worked in the canteen for twenty-eight years. Led the cafeteria transformation three years ago that increased customers by 30% and revenue by 40%.
