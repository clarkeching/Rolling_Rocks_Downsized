# The Inverted Pyramid

## Plain English Explanation
Steve's own discovery, triggered by seeing a newspaper folded with the headline above the fold. In the nineteenth century, journalists invented this technique because telegraph transmissions were unreliable—a 300-word article often only partially transmitted. They solved it by inverting articles: most newsworthy paragraph (the 'lede') first, then next most important, and so on. Even partial transmission was publishable.
