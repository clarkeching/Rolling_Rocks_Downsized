# The French Fry Revelation (Replenish on Demand)

## Plain English Explanation
The puzzle: why do they never run out of chips AND rarely throw any away? Two unspoken rules: don't run out, don't serve cold chips. The answer: serving staff watch the supply, and when it gets low, they shout and the kitchen cooks more. Craig calls this 'replenish on demand.' They cook what's selling, not what they predicted would sell. Result: 30% more customers, 40% more revenue.
