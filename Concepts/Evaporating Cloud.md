# The Evaporating Cloud

## Plain English Explanation
A thinking tool—a simple diagram—for solving significant problems. Five boxes arranged like a person: D and D' are the hands (conflicting options), B and C are the shoulders (concerns or needs threatened by each option), and A is the head (highest level goals). By examining the underlying needs rather than the surface options, you can often find solutions that satisfy both sides.
