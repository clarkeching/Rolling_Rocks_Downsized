# The Quality-Speed Trade-off (False Assumption)

## Plain English Explanation
The conventional wisdom that you must choose between quality and speed. This assumption drives behaviors like skipping testing and shipping buggy products. The entire book challenges this, demonstrating that quality done right actually increases speed.

## Who Teaches It
*This is the starting assumption to be challenged, not taught by anyone*
