# The Dog Food Problem

## Plain English Explanation
Making products you wouldn't use yourself. The cafeteria food was 'barely fit for human consumption—you wouldn't feed it to a dog' because no one ever tasted it before serving. The staff followed recipes but didn't care about the result. The solution: Craig insisted they eat the same food they served customers. This gave them a reason to care about quality.
