# Good Enough to Ship

## Plain English Explanation
Each small batch must be complete enough that it could ship, even though you won't ship until the deadline. The code, documentation, and everything else for each chunk must be fully tested, fully reworked, and good enough quality that you could ship it if needed. This provides a safety net—if the project is cut short, you have a working product with fewer features rather than nothing.
