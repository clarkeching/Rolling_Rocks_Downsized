# Small Batches

## Plain English Explanation
Reducing the size of work batches to improve quality and flexibility. Craig plants the idea with a paradoxical instruction: 'try not to think about small batches.' Cheryl describes it like planting grit in an oyster, hoping it becomes a pearl. She spent days trying NOT to think about it, then figured out how to cook vegetables in small batches. The first day was nuts, the second less nuts, and things kept improving.
